THE FOUNDER OF ICST

The International Campus of Science and Technology (ICST) University was founded by Dr. M. L. A. M. Hizbullah, who also served as the former Eastern Province Governor, according to ICST's website. He initiated the university with the goal of providing higher education opportunities, particularly for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, and to address the evolving demands of the labor market. Dr. Hizbullah's vision included a strong commitment to social responsibility and inclusivity in higher education.
